Research

Master's Thesis: Develop a black-box safe optimization algorithm

Yang Wang, Baiwei Guo, Giancarlo Ferrari Trecate

Online optimization for unknown dynamic systems recently attracts increasing attention. Safety during online optimization process becomes critical in various fields of application, e.g. robotic platforms, biomedical applications, electric nets, etc. We design a black-box safe optimization algorithm for potentially non-linear, non-convex systems extending a line search optimization scheme. The algorithm enjoys scalability and fast convergence speed.

Project: Map exploration for mobile robots in unknown environments

Yang Wang

Developed a map exploration algorithm for mobile robots in unknown environments, optimized the exploration performance by maximizing a designed reward function and implemented the algorithm in simulations on ROS.

Project: UR3 robotic arm for chess gaming
