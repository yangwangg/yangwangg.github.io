Research

Master's Thesis: Model-Free Safe Exploration and Optimization

Yang Wang, Baiwei Guo, Giancarlo Ferrari Trecate

Online optimization for unknown dynamical systems recently attracts increasing attention. Safety during online optimization process becomes critical in various fields of application, such as biomedical treatments, electric nets, robotic platforms, etc. We designed a black-box safe optimization algorithm for potentially non-linear, non-convex optimization problems extending the conventional line-search optimization scheme. The algorithm enjoys scalability and fast convergence.

Research assistant: Benchmarking a Neural ODE prediction model

Jonas Weigand, Yang Wang

Integrated multiple identification benchmarks into a continuous-time neural network prediction model for learning dynamical systems. The prediction model learns the system dynamics with dense neural networks and predicts states of dynamical systems utilizing an Eular or Runge-Kutta integrator.

Dynamic movement primitives - Learning movement from demonstration

Yang Wang, Min Wu

Studied the Dynamic Movement Primitives (DMPs) framework for movement reproduction and generalization and proposed an online obstacle avoidance strategy integrated into the traditional DMPs formulation with a dynamic potenfial field. [PDF]

Project: Map exploration for mobile robots in unknown environments

Yang Wang, Chengli Liu, Zhaoqi Li

Developed a map exploration algorithm for mobile robots in unknown environments. Optimized the exploration performance by designing and optimizing a reward function and implemented the algorithm in simulations on ROS.

Project: UR3 robotic arm for chess gaming

Yang Wang, Raphael Atlan

Developed a UR3 robotic arm control system for playing chess games, including designing a sensing module with a depth camera, a motion planning and collision check module, and communication and action execution modules. [PDF]
