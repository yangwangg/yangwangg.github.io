Master's Thesis: Develop a black-box safe optimization algorithm

Yang Wang, Baiwei Guo, Giancarlo Ferrari Trecate

Online optimization for unknown dynamic systems recently attracts increasing attention. Safety during the online optimization process becomes critical in various fields of application, e.g. robotic platforms, biomedical applications, electric nets, etc. We design a black-box safe optimization algorithm for potentially non-linear, non-convex systems extending the line search scheme. The algorithm enjoys scalability and fast convergence speed.
